About us

About Us

MedLab Pharmacy is a full-service community pharmacy located in sunny Tamarac, Florida. We are the closest pharmacy to University Community Hospital and it’s state-of-the-art Medical Center campus.

In addition to dispensing prescription medication, our pharmacists regularly perform Medication Therapy Management consultations for patients with multiple medical conditions and more complex medication profiles.

We are a family oriented facility and can fill prescriptions for every member of your household including children, adults and seniors, from pediatrics to geriatrics and for all medical specialties.

We pride ourselves in providing our patients a more professional atmosphere than other places where prescriptions can be filled like grocery stores, large chain drugstores, drive-thru places, or other places where pharmacy and patient care are not their number one priority.

Patients of MedLab Pharmacy are invited to sit in our waiting room as they would in a doctor’s office while their prescriptions are being filled, or if they are within a 5 mile radius have their medication delivered to their door.

While at our Pharmacy, MedLab patients can consult with an experienced pharmacist who will go over all their medications in a professional manner, and discuss ways to enhance their compliance and make other lifestyle adjustments to help their health and longevity.

MedLab Pharmacy has accounts with many of the largest and most reliable suppliers of Prescription Medications in the world. In addition, we are able to supply all members you and all other members of your house with the world’s finest Non-Prescription Drugs, Medical Supplies, and Nutritional Supplements.

Non-Narcotic Pain Solutions

Opioids or narcotics can be extremely addictive and in some cases fatal. At Med Lab Pharmacy we want to work with your primary health care provider to offer you non-narcotic pain solutions. We have a wide selection of options and offer free counseling to find the option that best suits your needs:

  • Topical pain relievers: creams and ointments that contain medication can be applied directly to the skin.
  • Non-opioid prescription drugs: these medications include triptans used for headaches or migraines
  • Over-the-counter medications: pain relievers that don’t require a prescription can aid in relieving mild to moderate pain symptoms.

Talk to your health care professional about which option works best for you and then come see us at Med Lab Pharmacy.

Skin Solutions

The skin is the body’s largest organ, so taking proper care of it is vital. The Med Lab Pharmacy staff is here to provide you with all your skin solution needs, from preventative care to treatments. Acne is the most common skin condition, to prevent breakouts pick up one of our noncomedogenic or nonacnegenic face wash products that won’t clog your pores. If you are experiencing an acne breakout talk to one of our pharmacists about what treatment option is best for you.

  • Non-prescription acne treatments: for mild blemishes or non-inflammatory acne, ask your pharmacist about a non-prescription acne treatment containing Salicylic acid. These treatments will unclog the pores.
  • Prescription acne treatments: for more serious breakouts containing cysts or nodules our staff recommends seeing a dermatologist. Once you have been recommended a prescription acne treatment we will be happy to provide you with what you need.

Diabetes Supply Specialists

Living with diabetes can be challenging and requires constant monitoring. Here at Med Lab Pharmacy we have stocked our store with all the tools you require to keep your diabetes under control:

  • AIC self check tests
  • Urine ketone test strips
  • Blood ketone test strips
  • Insulin coolers
  • Insulin
  • Glucose tablets

Any supplies you need that we don't have on hand can be requested through our diabetes supply specialist, who will ensure you have everything you need.

Medication Therapy Management  (MTM)

We all have questions about our medications now and then, but medication therapy management (MTM) takes this a step further. MTM is “a service or group of services that optimize therapeutic outcomes for individual patients,” not all illnesses or medications can be monitored with a simple in house counseling session. Talk to your pharmacist to see if you may require MTM, this involves a comprehensive treatment and review process, wellness plans and consultations to make sure your medication is working properly. For any questions concerning MTM talk to any of our staff in person or over the phone
